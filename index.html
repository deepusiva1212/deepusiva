About Us

About Deepu Siva
Private Limited

Deepu Siva Private Limited is a premier engineering and fabrication company proudly based in Coimbatore. Founded on a legacy of industrial expertise going back to D S Electricals (2017), we have grown into a focused, modern fabrication enterprise led by Rajavel Palaniyappan and Deepadharsan Rajavel.

We specialise in metal fabrication, industrial roofing, solar mounting structures and structural construction — serving factories, warehouses, solar developers and industrial clients across Tamil Nadu and Karnataka.
