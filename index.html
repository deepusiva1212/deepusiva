About Us

About Deepu Siva
Private Limited

Building on a Legacy of Resilience and Expertise Welcome to Deepu Siva Private Limited, a premier engineering and fabrication company proudly based in Coimbatore. We specialize in delivering robust, precision-engineered structural solutions, focusing on industrial welding, roof sheeting, street light installations, and comprehensive solar fabrication. While Deepu Siva Private Limited is a newly incorporated venture, our roots in the industrial sector run deep, built upon years of hands-on experience, hard work, and family dedication. Our Origins: The D S Electricals Journey The foundation of our expertise was laid on July 1, 2017, by our founder, Mr. Rajavel Palaniyappan. Driven by an unwavering entrepreneurial spirit and a commitment to overcoming early business challenges, he established D S Electricals in Coimbatore. The company began its journey by supplying high-quality compressor motor fans and essential spare parts, quickly earning a reputation for reliability and hard work in the local industrial community. Embracing the Future of Energy By 2024, recognizing the rapid growth and critical importance of renewable energy, the business strategically pivoted. Collaborating with trusted industry partners like Mr. Saravana, the company expanded into the solar energy sector. What began as dedicated job work steadily transformed into a larger operation, taking on complex orders and building a strong foundation in solar structural requirements. The Next Chapter: Deepu Siva Private Limited Today, that legacy of adaptability, resilience, and industrial knowledge has evolved into Deepu Siva Private Limited. Founded by the father-son leadership team of Rajavel Palaniyappan and Deepadharsan Rajavel, this company represents the consolidation of our extensive experience into a focused, modern fabrication enterprise. We bring the same dedication that built D S Electricals into every project we undertake today. Whether it is a large-scale roof sheeting project, custom welding work, or the structural backbone for a new solar grid, we are committed to providing top-tier craftsmanship, durable materials, and exceptional service to our clients.

From manufacturing precision-fabricated steel components to building large-scale industrial roofing systems and solar mounting structures, our team is committed to quality, safety, and on-time delivery. We serve factories, warehouses, solar developers, and construction firms across Tamil Nadu and Karnataka.
