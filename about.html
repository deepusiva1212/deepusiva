Our Legacy & Vision

A Foundation of Trust

The journey of Deepu Siva Private Limited is built upon a strong foundation of hard work, engineering precision, and unwavering dedication to quality. Tracing our roots back to the establishment of D.S. Electricals, we have spent years mastering the complexities of industrial project execution.

Today, as a fully incorporated Private Limited company based in Coimbatore, we have expanded our expertise to become a leading force in heavy metal fabrication, industrial roofing, and advanced solar mounting structures.

Our transition from a trusted local enterprise to a comprehensive corporate engineering partner reflects our commitment to scaling our operations while maintaining the family-driven values of integrity and client satisfaction.
